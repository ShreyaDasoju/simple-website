AVOCADO PESTO PASTA RECIPE

Serves 2

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1/2 cup of fresh basil leaves
  • 2-3 cloves of fresh garlic or 1 1/2 tsp of garlic powder
  • 3 tsp lemon/lime juice
  • 1/2 tsp table salt
  • 2 1/2 tbsp olive oil
  • black pepper and chili pepper flakes to taste
  • pasta of your choosing

Preparation:

  1. Boil pasta of your choosing until almost fully cooked
  2. In a food processor, add your avocado, basil leaves, garlic, lime juice, salt, olive oil, and other seasoning. Blend until creamy, and add olive oil in a slow stream if needed.
  3. Heat up pan and add sauce and pasta together and mix
  4. Add pasta to your serving dish, and top with a little parmesan if you like
  5. Enjoy!

HONEY SRIRACHA TOFU RECIPE

Serves 2

Ingredients:

  • 1 block of firm/medium firm tofu
  • 1 tsp dark soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp sriracha
  • 1 tbsp chili garlic sauce
  • 1 tsp of gochujang
  • 6 tbsp water
  • cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup of olive/vegetable oil

Preparation:

  1. Cut tofu block into cubes
  2. Toss tofu cubes in enough cornstarch to lightly coat them
  3. Fry tofu until golden brown
  4. In a bowl, mix the water, soy sauce, honey, sriracha, chili garlic sauce, and gochujang together
  5. Once the tofu is cooked, add the sauce into the pan and cook together
  6. Place tofu in serving dish and serve with sesame seeds and chopped green onions
  7. Serve with jasmine rice or any rice of your choosing
  8. Enjoy!

Veggie Chow Mein

Serves 3

Ingredients:

  • Chow mein noodles (3 blocks)
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, 1/2 green bell pepper
  • 1 pack of mushrooms
  • 2 cups of thinly diced cabbage
  • 1 1/2 tbsp of brown sugar
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp of sriracha
  • 2 tbsp of chili garlic sauce
  • 1 tsp of gochujang
  • 2 tsp of oyster sauce
  • add black pepper, salt, and garlic powder for taste

Preparation:

  1. In a pan, add brown sugar and once it starts to bubble, add your cabbage
  2. Push the cabbage to the side, add some oil to the pan and sautee the rest of your vegetables
  3. Boil your noodles until fooly cooked
  4. Add the cooked noodles to the sauteed vegetables
  5. Add your soy sauce, sriracha, child garlic sauce, gochujang, oyster sauce, salt, pepper, and garlic powder
  6. Once everything is combined, move to serving dish
  7. Garnish with sesame seeds and green onions
